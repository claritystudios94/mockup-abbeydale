The clinical team

Who you'll actually see

At Abbeydale, you see the same dentist at every appointment. Here's who that is.

RA
Dr Raad Abdulhamed
Dentist — MSc Endodontics · PhD Restorative Dentistry

Qualifying in 1998, Dr Abdulhamed serves as a Clinical Teacher at the University of Sheffield and Clinical Supervisor at Charles Clifford Dental Hospital. An internationally renowned clinician who lectures and supervises PhD students worldwide.

SO
Dr Safa Othman
Dentist — BDS Hons, University of Sheffield

Graduating with Honours in 2017, Dr Othman specialises in cosmetic dentistry — including teeth whitening, composite bonding, and Invisalign. Also trained in facial aesthetics. Known for her calm, approachable manner.
