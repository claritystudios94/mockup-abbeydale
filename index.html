What we offer

Every smile is different

From your first nervous check-up to a complete transformation — we offer the full range of treatments in a calm, welcoming environment.

01

Private Dentistry

Comprehensive examinations, professional cleaning, personalised treatment plans, and digital X-rays. Gentle, unhurried care for patients of all ages.

New patients welcome
02

Dental Implants

The gold standard for replacing missing teeth. Implants look, feel and function like your natural teeth — and with proper care, they last a lifetime.

Permanent solution
03

Orthodontics

Straighter teeth aren't just about looks. Better alignment makes cleaning easier and reduces the risk of decay and gum disease over time.

Invisalign available
04

Teeth Whitening

Embrace a natural shade of white with results you'll see within two weeks. Long-lasting, with only a small annual top-up needed to maintain your result.

From home or in-surgery

A quick, painless way to reshape, repair, or lengthen teeth. No drilling required in most cases — results in a single visit.

Same-day results

The people behind your care

Meet our team

Every member of our team is chosen for their warmth as much as their expertise. We believe the people caring for you matter just as much as the treatments we offer.

RA
Dr Raad Abdulhamed
Dentist — MSc Endodontics · PhD Restorative Dentistry

Qualifying in 1998, Dr Abdulhamed has built a career spanning primary and secondary care and dental education. He serves as a Clinical Teacher in Restorative Dentistry at the University of Sheffield School of Clinical Dentistry, and as a Clinical Supervisor at Charles Clifford Dental Hospital. An internationally renowned clinician, he lectures and supervises PhD students worldwide — bringing exceptional expertise to every patient at Abbeydale.

SO
Dr Safa Othman
Dentist — BDS Hons, University of Sheffield

Graduating with Honours from the University of Sheffield in 2017, Dr Othman specialises in cosmetic dentistry — including teeth whitening, composite bonding, white fillings, and Invisalign. She is also trained in facial aesthetics, including wrinkle softening and dermal fillers. Known for her relaxed and approachable manner, Dr Othman's goal is not just to restore confident smiles, but to make every visit genuinely comfortable.

Two University of Sheffield-trained dentists — including a PhD-level specialist who teaches the next generation of dentists. This is who's looking after your teeth.
